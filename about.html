About SafetyNet Foundation

We are the first DeSo-registered Foundation at the Dutch Chamber of Commerce (KvK 97663069). Our mission is to strengthen the DeSo ecosystem with redundancy, transparency, and community-owned infrastructure.

What we do

  • Operate validators and supporting services (e.g., GraphQL) with geographic redundancy.
  • Protect user content and availability through community-run infrastructure.
  • Collaborate with node operators and developers to grow the ecosystem.
  • Stress-test resilience and add on-chain functionality.
